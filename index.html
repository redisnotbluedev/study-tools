Study Tips

  • Active Recall: Test yourself on the material instead of just re-reading.
  • Spaced Repetition: Review information at increasing intervals.
  • Pomodoro Technique: Work for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break.
  • Mind Mapping: Visualize connections between concepts.
  • Healthy Habits: Get enough sleep, eat well, and exercise regularly.
